Career

Computer Engineers research, design, develop, and test computer systems and components, and discover new directions in hardware for computers, leading to rapid advances in the field of computer technology.


Median annual wage is about $131,874 per year and $63.40 per hour in 2016. Lowest 10% earned less than $65K, while highest 10% earned $167K+. Lifetime income for 40 years predicted to be $5,274,960.


They usually work in research laboratories building and testing computer models in high-tech manufacturing firms.
